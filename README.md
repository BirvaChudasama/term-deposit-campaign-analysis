# Term Deposit Campaign Analysis

## Overview
This project presents an executive-level Power BI dashboard analyzing the performance of a bank’s telephonic marketing campaign for term deposits. The goal is to identify high-conversion customer segments and provide actionable targeting insights to improve campaign efficiency and ROI.

## Business Objective
- Measure overall campaign success
- Identify customer demographics with higher conversion likelihood
- Analyze customer product ownership (loans, housing)
- Recommend data-driven targeting strategies for future campaigns

## Dataset
- **Source:** Banking Dataset – Marketing Targets (Kaggle)
- **File:** Train.csv
- Contains customer demographics, contact history, product ownership, and campaign outcomes

## Dashboard Structure
The Power BI report consists of **two pages** designed for executive consumption:

### 1️⃣ Overview
- Campaign Success Rate (KPI)
- Total Customers & Successful Subscriptions
- Average Balance of Subscribed Customers
- Conversion Rate by:
  - Education
  - Job Type
  - Marital Status

### 2️⃣ Targeting Insights
- Impact of Previous Campaign Outcome on Conversion
- Conversion Rate vs. Number of Call Attempts
- Priority Customer Segments Table with conditional formatting
- Insights on loan and housing product ownership

## Key Insights
- Customers with fewer call attempts (1–3) show higher conversion rates
- Previous successful campaign interactions significantly increase conversion likelihood
- Customers without active loans or housing products convert at higher rates
- Certain demographic segments (education level, job category) consistently outperform others
